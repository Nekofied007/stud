# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: November 9, 2025

## Introduction

STUD ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

## Data We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Email address** (for account creation)
- **Password** (hashed, never stored in plain text)
- **Course progress** (videos watched, quiz scores)
- **Questions asked to AI tutor** (for improving service)

### Automatically Collected
- **Usage data** (pages visited, features used)
- **Device information** (browser type, OS)
- **IP address** (for rate limiting and security)

### Data We Do NOT Collect
- We do NOT collect payment information (handled by third-party processors)
- We do NOT sell your data to third parties
- We do NOT track you across other websites

## How We Use Your Data

- **Provide the service**: Course creation, progress tracking, AI tutoring
- **Improve the product**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
- **Security**: Detect and prevent abuse, fraud, or security threats
- **Communication**: Send important service updates (opt-in for marketing)

## Data Sharing

We share data only in these limited cases:

- **YouTube API**: We query YouTube for public playlist data (subject to YouTube TOS)
- **OpenAI**: We send transcripts and questions to OpenAI for AI features
- **Cloud providers**: AWS/DigitalOcean host our infrastructure
- **Legal requirements**: If required by law or to protect rights

We do NOT sell your data. Third-party services have their own privacy policies.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** your data (download a copy)
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your account and data
- **Object** to processing (e.g., opt out of analytics)
- **Data portability** (export in JSON format)

To exercise these rights, email privacy@stud.education

## Data Retention

- **Active accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Deleted accounts**: Data deleted within 30 days (except legal obligations)
- **Backups**: Backup copies deleted within 90 days

## Security

We implement security measures including:

- **Encryption in transit** (HTTPS/TLS)
- **Encrypted passwords** (bcrypt hashing)
- **API key protection** (environment variables, not in code)
- **Rate limiting** (prevent abuse)
- **Regular security audits**

No system is 100% secure. Report vulnerabilities to security@stud.education

## Cookies & Tracking

- **Essential cookies**: Required for login and functionality
- **Analytics**: Optional (can opt out in settings)
- **Third-party cookies**: We do not use third-party tracking

## Children's Privacy
